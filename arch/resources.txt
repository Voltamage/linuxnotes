TROUBLESHOOTING
chroot back into the system by mounting the files and arch-chroot
    swapon  /dev/sda2
    mount   /dev/sda3   /mnt
    mount   /dev/sda1   /mnt/boot/efi
    mount   /dev/sda4   /mnt/home
    arch-chroot /mnt
    
deleting a folder
    rm -r

deleting a file
    rm
